Our Dream

SUSTAINABILITY ON THE RIVER

YOU CAN CALL ME QUEEN BEE

Jane: Proud Mama, Herder of chickens, farm hands & cats!

Hi, my name is Jane Lewis. I'm the matriarch of a 26 acre legacy farm on the Northeast Cape Fear River in New Hanover County, NC. Our goal is to transform this family land into a modest, sustainable agricultural enterprise with room for special events hosting: everything from musical gatherings, to art festivals, to traditional dance, to food and wine galas!

Peach Orchard, Mushrooms, Watermelons

Our goal for the inaugural growing season includes planting a small peach orchard, a loblolly pine mushroom stand, and a seasonal watermelon grove.

Our orchard flora selections will be informed by sustainable agriculture concerns, market feasibility, data from North Carolina State's Agriculture Extension, and soil & drainage tests.

We also plan to build two new sheds, an outdoor stage, a camping and event space, and finish a converted Tobacco Barn guest house!
