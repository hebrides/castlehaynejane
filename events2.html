What's Your Dream Event?

As we build our farm, we want to know what you'd like to do here!

We envision large, tented outdoor parties, barn dances, corporate retreats, celebratory dinners & more. What's your dream event? We want to make that happen for you! We plan three event stages, multiple covered barns and greenhouses, rows of fruit trees and grape trellises, beautiful stays at our farmhouses, and plenty of scenic spots for once in a lifetime memories!

As we build out the farm we want to gauge the demand for particular facilities and services.

We look forward to hosting a variety of events, including business, spiritual, and life celebrations, music festivals, traditional dances, fairs, and retreats. We will provide visitors for all occasions a unique opportunity to experience our special farm amenities.
