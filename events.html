What's Your Dream Event?

We envision large, tented outdoor parties, barn dances, corporate retreats, celebratory dinners & more. What's your dream event? We want to make that happen for you! We plan three event stages, multiple covered barns and greenhouses, rows of fruit trees and grape trellises, beautiful stays at our farmhouses, and plenty of scenic spots for once in a lifetime memories!

Our Farm Plan

The farm map highlights future barns, event lawns, vineyards, orchards, and cozy gathering spaces that will anchor the experiences we host.

Space for ceremonies, dinners, retreats, and workshops will be surrounded by fruit trees and grape trellises, with the river breeze keeping everything cool. We are designing every corner to feel intentional, refined, and deeply connected to the heritage of Castle Hayne.
