What You Get

Lifetime access and one unforgettable weekend that's all yours.

Benefactors are the backbone of the build-out. Your $10,000 contribution comes back to you in lifetime camping privileges, seasonal event passes every year we operate, and — most uniquely — one free weekend rental of the entire farm and its facilities to host an event of your own choosing.

We'll help you make the magic: organize logistics, coordinate service providers, and put our knowledge of the space to work for your celebration. You lead the vision; we run the venue.

  • Lifetime camping on the farm
  • All-access seasonal event passes (renewed annually)
  • One free weekend farm rental to host your own event
  • Dedicated weekend coordination support from Jane's team
  • Benefactor recognition on the farm's supporter wall
  • Behind-the-scenes farm progress updates
  • Priority reservations on all future bookings

Where Your Money Goes

Benefactor funding builds the event infrastructure.

At $10,000, your contribution funds major event infrastructure: the main barn conversion, stage flooring, electrical and lighting runs, restroom facilities, and the kind of structural improvements that make the farm a real venue — not just a pretty field.

This tier single-handedly moves the farm from planning to operational.
