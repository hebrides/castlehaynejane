What You Get

A week at Jane's Campground — on us.

Your $100 contribution gives you a free one-week stay at our HipCamp-style campground on the farm. Pitch your tent steps from the Northeast Cape Fear River, walk the orchards in the morning, and go to sleep knowing your money is working on something real.

This is our entry-level tier — the best way to become a founding supporter without a major commitment, and the perfect excuse to finally visit.

  • One free week at Jane's Campground (redeemable 2026 season)
  • Access to farm walking trails and orchard areas
  • Farm supporter badge — your name in our founding supporter list
  • First access to future event announcements

Where Your Money Goes

Every dollar goes straight to the build-out.

We're pre-revenue — still clearing, planting, and constructing. Your support at this stage has outsized impact. Contributions at the Happy Camper level collectively fund things like seedlings for the peach orchard, fencing on the east pasture, and supplies for the campsite itself.

We publish a full fund breakdown on our Support page so you can see exactly how contributions are allocated.
