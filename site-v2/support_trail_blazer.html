What You Get

12 months of access. Every event, every season.

The Trail Blazer tier gives you free camping for an entire year — come and go as you please throughout the 2026-2027 season — plus passes to every farm event we host during that time. Barn dances, music festivals, seasonal dinners, gallery nights: you're on the list for all of it.

This tier is for the people who believe in the project enough to show up more than once. And as early supporters, Trail Blazers get first pick of dates and event priority over general admission.

  • Free camping for one full year (2026–2027 season)
  • Passes to every farm-hosted event during your year
  • Priority booking for camping dates over general admission
  • Trail Blazer recognition in our founding supporter wall
  • Farm progress updates with behind-the-scenes access

Where Your Money Goes

Trail Blazers fund the infrastructure.

At $1,000, your contribution directly funds mid-scale infrastructure: the event stage platform, campsite clearing and leveling, utility runs to the east barn, and fencing for the orchard perimeter. These are the physical bones of what becomes a working venue.
