The Setting

26 acres. A working farm. A river at the edge.

The Northeast Cape Fear River borders the property on two sides. The farm itself includes open meadows, a peach orchard, and multiple planned venues — a main barn, an outdoor stage, and riverbank gathering areas. It's an environment you can't replicate in a hotel ballroom or a rented vineyard.

The farm is being designed from the ground up with events in mind. Every structure is being built with hosting in mind — acoustics, flow, lighting, catering infrastructure. If you sponsor now, you'll have a hand in shaping the space that hosts your event.

  • Authentic working farm setting — not a stage set
  • River access and open-air gathering areas
  • Custom layout possible during build-out
  • Brand alignment with sustainability and local agriculture
  • Story-rich environment that turns events into experiences
Founding Rates

Lock in your event before we open.

The farm won't be open to the public until 2027. Sponsors who commit during the build-out phase get two things that won't exist later: preferred pricing and preferred dates. Once the venue opens, high-demand weekends will go fast. Founding sponsors choose first.

This isn't a deposit-and-wait situation either. We'll work with you now to understand your vision, design the space around your event type, and lock in what the farm owes you in writing.

What's Included

The farm handles the setting. You bring the reason.

  • Full use of designated event spaces (barn, stage, outdoor areas)
  • Basic lighting and power infrastructure
  • On-site parking and farm access roads
  • Farm staff available for event day coordination

  • Farm tour and story integration available on request
