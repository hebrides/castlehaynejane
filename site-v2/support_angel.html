What You Get

Naming rights. Advisory seat. Lifetime everything.

Angel supporters are founding partners of the farm in the fullest sense. Your $100,000 contribution underwrites major phases of the build-out — and in return, you receive the most comprehensive benefits we offer: lifetime organized events, lifetime camping, naming rights to one barn or event space of your choosing, and a permanent seat on our board of advisors.

This isn't a donation. It's a stake. Your name — or whoever you'd like to honor — will be on the structure you help build, as long as the farm stands.

  • Lifetime yearly events of your choice (we organize, you design the vision)
  • Lifetime camping — come whenever you like, for as long as you like
  • Naming rights to one barn or event space at the farm
  • Permanent seat on the Farm's Board of Advisors
  • Co-creator recognition in all farm publications and signage
  • Dedicated direct line of communication with Jane and David
  • First right of refusal on any future investment opportunities

The Impact

A single Angel investment transforms the farm.

At $100,000, a single Angel contribution moves the farm from an active build-out into a fully operational venue. This level of support funds the complete fit-out of the main event barn, the outdoor stage and covered amphitheater, the restored Tobacco Barn guest house, and the infrastructure for a permanent campground.

It also positions the farm for long-term sustainability — not dependent on season-by-season revenue, but structured from a foundation of real capital investment. We approach this relationship with full transparency, including access to financials, planning documents, and strategic decisions.
