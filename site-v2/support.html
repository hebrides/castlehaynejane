    Why This Is Different

    This is an early investment in a real farm — not a donation.

    The farm is in active build-out on 26 acres on the Northeast Cape Fear River. Every dollar goes to infrastructure: clearing land, building barns, planting orchards, and creating the event facilities that will generate revenue for years to come.

    In return, you're not donating — you're taking a position. Your tier is a binding commitment the farm will honor: a week of camping, a year of event passes, facility access for your own events, or a permanent advisory seat. These are pre-revenue prices that will not exist once the farm opens.
