    Why It Matters

    Every dollar builds something you can visit, celebrate on, and be proud of.

    The farm is pre-revenue — we're in the build-out phase. Supporter contributions go directly toward infrastructure: clearing land, building barns, planting orchards, and creating the event facilities that will sustain everything long-term.

    In return, you're not just donating — you're buying in. Your tier determines what the farm owes you: a night's camping, a year of event passes, or a seat at the table as an advisor.
